What this tracks

On March 12, 2025, EPA announced what it called the “biggest deregulatory action in U.S. history.” This tracker pulls the actual regulations.gov docket record to show what was filed — and what wasn't — since Inauguration Day. It covers State and Tribal Implementation Plans, NAAQS standards, emission rules (MATS, NESHAPs, NSPS), vehicle greenhouse gas standards, the Good Neighbor Plan, Regional Haze, and consent decrees.
