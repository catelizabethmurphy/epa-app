Nebraska drinking-water testing

4 of 53 Nebraska water systems reported PFAS above the EPA standards Biden set in 2024.

8% of public water systems in Nebraska that EPA sampled for PFAS reported at least one result above the standards Biden's EPA finalized in 2024 for PFOA, PFOS, PFHxS, PFNA, or HFPO-DA (GenX). The Trump EPA is now moving to rescind those standards for every PFAS except PFOA and PFOS.

What is Nebraska doing about PFAS?

As the federal drinking-water standards retreat, states are filling the gap with their own restrictions on PFAS in consumer products, food packaging, firefighting foam, and industrial discharges.
