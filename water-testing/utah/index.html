Utah drinking-water testing

5 of 129 Utah water systems reported PFAS above the EPA standards Biden set in 2024.

What is Utah doing about PFAS?

As the federal drinking-water standards retreat, states are filling the gap with their own restrictions on PFAS in consumer products, food packaging, firefighting foam, and industrial discharges.

Source: EPA Fifth Unregulated Contaminant Monitoring Rule (UCMR 5). "Worst exceedance" shows how many times above the EPA standard the system's highest sample registered, and which compound it was.
