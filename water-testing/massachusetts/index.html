Massachusetts drinking-water testing

Massachusetts is among the worst states in the country for PFAS in drinking water.

41% of public water systems in Massachusetts that EPA sampled for PFAS reported at least one result above the standards Biden's EPA finalized in 2024 for PFOA, PFOS, PFHxS, PFNA, or HFPO-DA (GenX). The Trump EPA is now moving to rescind those standards for every PFAS except PFOA and PFOS.
