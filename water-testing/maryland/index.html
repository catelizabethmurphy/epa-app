Maryland drinking-water testing

16 of 83 Maryland water systems reported PFAS above the EPA standards Biden set in 2024.

19% of public water systems in Maryland that EPA sampled for PFAS reported at least one result above the standards Biden's EPA finalized in 2024 for PFOA, PFOS, PFHxS, PFNA, or HFPO-DA (GenX). The Trump EPA is now moving to rescind those standards for every PFAS except PFOA and PFOS.

What is Maryland doing about PFAS?

As the federal drinking-water standards retreat, states are filling the gap with their own restrictions on PFAS in consumer products, food packaging, firefighting foam, and industrial discharges.

Source: EPA Fifth Unregulated Contaminant Monitoring Rule (UCMR 5). "Worst exceedance" shows how many times above the EPA standard the system's highest sample registered, and which compound it was.
