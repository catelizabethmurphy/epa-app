New Hampshire drinking-water testing

New Hampshire is among the worst states in the country for PFAS in drinking water.

31% of public water systems in New Hampshire that EPA sampled for PFAS reported at least one result above the standards Biden's EPA finalized in 2024 for PFOA, PFOS, PFHxS, PFNA, or HFPO-DA (GenX). The Trump EPA is now moving to rescind those standards for every PFAS except PFOA and PFOS.

What is New Hampshire doing about PFAS?

As the federal drinking-water standards retreat, states are filling the gap with their own restrictions on PFAS in consumer products, food packaging, firefighting foam, and industrial discharges.
